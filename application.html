Explore the Game: Super Mini Racing

About the Game

  • Race your top rivals in 10 different challenging levels in the best vehicle you can get!
  • Buy new vehicles to become the fastest driver on the road, using the arrow keys to drive and steer.
  • Finish first to unlock the next level!
  • Play alone, or race alongside friends in a showdown!

How to Play

Follow these instructions to master the game:

  • WASD: FWD/BKWD/LFT/RGT
  • 'R' Key: Respawn
  • Airtime & Drifting: POINTS
